Tower Ridge Condos

Oconomowoc

Tower Ridge Condos is located in Oconomowoc, WI. Oconomowoc is a welcoming community with a historic downtown. The name Oconomowoc was derived from the Potowatomi Indian term for “waterfall.” Downtown Oconomowoc has historic 19th-century architecture, grand Victorian homes, and beautiful walking areas.

Our Community

Tower Ridge Condominium Development is locally owned and managed. You won’t have to deal with layers of management. “One call, text, or email does it all.” Tower Ridge Condominiums are nestled into a quiet subdivision with beautiful, paved, walking paths. Plenty of gorgeous views, privacy with quick access to freeways. About 5 minutes to I-94. You are going to be happy here.

Your Spot
